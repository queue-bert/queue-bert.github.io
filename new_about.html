A Little About Me!

Here's a picture of me and Jared Polis! I worked as a canvasser when he was campaigning a while back and when he came into the office, he was more than happy to take a picture with me. His election campaign was immensely important to me, not only as a queer man myself, but also as a first-generation American and first-generation college student!

I think this picture also just speaks to my dedication to being involed in issues that I care about. Admittedly, my focus on societal issues shifts every few years but I still try my best to make a change. Right now, an issue I'm particularly invested in is queer and women's health especially since I plan to be an advocate for these issues when I'm a doctor in the future! In fact, this summer, I have an internship with the NYAGI project which will involve me creating software to teach midwives and other pre-natal care specialists in remote regions of the world how to use portable ultrasound to detect birth complications that will result in childbirth death. More than 300,000 women a year die from treatable birth complications so it's going to be awesome making software that saves lives!
