Add A Button

The numbers for the buttons already created below correspond to known notes and their frequencies. A quick Google search should tell you what frequency goes with the particular note that you'd like to add. Otherwise, feel free to add any number that you want! Try to play around with the buttons below to get a feel for what a good frequency would be to experiment with. Too high or too low of a frequency might make the sound unaudible if you don't happen to have great speakers on your device! Just a bit of warning before you explore.

About Tone.js

Tone.js is a Web Audio framework for creating interactive music in the browser. The architecture of Tone.js aims to be familiar to both musicians and audio programmers creating web-based audio applications. On the high-level, Tone offers common DAW (digital audio workstation) features like a global transport for synchronizing and scheduling events as well as prebuilt synths and effects. Additionally, Tone provides high-performance building blocks to create your own synthesizers, effects, and complex control signals. Click on Macintosh below to find out more!
